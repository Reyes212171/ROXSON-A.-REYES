Recipe 1: Step by Step
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • Butter
Instructions:
  1. Mix flour, eggs, sugar, and milk together.
  2. Heat the pan and add butter.
  3. Pour the mixture into the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
Recipe 2: Step by Step
Ingredients:
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 onion
  • Salt and pepper
Instructions:
  1. Cook rice according to package instructions.
  2. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, and onion.
  3. Grill the chicken for 5-7 minutes on each side.
  4. Serve the chicken over the rice.
Recipe 3: Step by Step
Ingredients:
  • 1 spaghetti squash
  • 2 cups of tomato sauce
  • 1 cup of mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
Instructions:
  1. Cut the spaghetti squash in half and remove the seeds.
  2. Roast the squash at 400°F for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat the tomato sauce and pour over the roasted squash.
  4. Top with mozzarella cheese and bake for another 5 minutes.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
